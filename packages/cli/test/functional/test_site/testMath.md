Stirling's approximation:

$$
\begin{aligned}
n! = \sqrt{2 \pi n} (\frac{n}{e})^n e^{\alpha_n} \\
where \\
\frac{1}{12n+1} < \alpha_n < \frac{1}{12n}
\end{aligned}
$$
